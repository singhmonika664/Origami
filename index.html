Interesting Facts about Camel

  1. Camels are social animals who roam the deserts in search of food and water.
  2. Camels have two rows of thick eyelashes to protect their eyes from the desert dust
  3. A thirsty camel can drink up to 135 liters in one sitting.

Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  1. Chameleons are a very unique branch of the lizard group of reptiles.
  2. Chameleons have amazing eyes. The bulging upper and lower eyelids are joined and the pupil peaks out from a pinhole sized gap.
  3. The chameleons eyes can rotate and focus separately on 180-degree arcs.This gives them a full 360-degree field of vision.

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. They can find their way back to their nests from 1300 miles away.

Interesting Facts about Teddy Bear

  1. The term bear-hug was first recorded in 1846
  2. The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906
  3. In 1902 in Germany, Steiff launched “Bear 55 PB”, the first toy bear with jointed arms and legs.

Interesting Facts about Panda

  1. They have great camouflage for their environment
  2. Pandas are BIG eaters – every day they fill their tummies for up to 12 hours
  3. Baby pandas are born pink and measure about 15cm-size of a pencil

Interesting Facts about Flying Cicada

  1. Cicadas are like tiny violins as their body is similar to that of a violin or a guitar
  2. Cicadas make noise like toy frogs
  3. They’re underground builders
